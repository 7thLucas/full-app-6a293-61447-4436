# Product Overview — Video Analyzer

## Working Title
Video Analyzer

## What It Is
A web application that lets users upload video files or paste a video URL and instantly receive a full AI-generated transcript plus smart content analysis. The app eliminates the manual work of watching, rewatching, and noting — drop in a video and walk away with structured, searchable, exportable content.

## Core Problem
Extracting useful information from video is slow and painful. Watching recordings in real-time, manually transcribing speech, and hunting for specific moments wastes hours. Existing transcription tools are either expensive enterprise products, technically complex to set up, or deliver poor accuracy. There is no frictionless, fast, all-in-one tool for the everyday user.

## Target Users
- **Content creators** — need transcripts for captions, repurposing content, or SEO metadata
- **Researchers & students** — processing lecture recordings, interviews, and documentary footage
- **Business professionals** — reviewing meeting recordings, webinars, and sales calls
- **Educators & media teams** — analyzing and archiving video content at scale

## Core Capabilities (MVP)
1. **Video Upload / URL Input** — Accept local video files (MP4, MOV, AVI, etc.) or a pasted online video URL
2. **AI Auto-Transcription** — Accurate, timestamped speech-to-text with speaker detection and punctuation
3. **Smart Video Analysis** — Topic detection, key moment identification, and sentiment analysis across the video
4. **Search & Navigate** — Full-text search within the transcript with one-click jump to that video timestamp
5. **Transcript Export** — Download as TXT, SRT, or VTT, or copy to clipboard instantly
6. **AI Summary** — Concise auto-generated summary of the video's content — without watching a second of it

## Positioning
AI-first video-to-text tool for anyone who needs to understand and repurpose video content fast — without an enterprise price tag or a technical setup. Fast, accurate, and effortless.

## Brand & Tone
Clear, efficient, and professional. The product does the heavy lifting; the user gets clean results. No jargon, no friction, no setup ceremony.

## Strategic Principles
- Zero configuration: no API keys, no installs, no onboarding friction
- Speed first: results in minutes, not hours
- Portability: transcripts export in standard formats, usable anywhere
